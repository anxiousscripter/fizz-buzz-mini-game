What is Fizz Buzz?

FizzBuzz is a simple word game that teaches you about division where you get to choose three things; the number you want to count up to, your Fizz number, and your Buzz number.

How It Works

We start by counting from one up to the number you choose. As we count, each number will be shown on screen. For this example, we'll choose 3 as our Fizz number and 5 as our Buzz number. If the number about to be shown can be divided by 3, it changes into the word 'Fizz'.

And if the number about to be shown can be divided by 5, it changes into the word 'Buzz'. All other numbers stay the same, so you can follow along as the pattern builds.

And When It Can Be Divided By Both?

When the number about to be shown can be divided by both your Fizz and your Buzz, it makes a FizzBuzz!

Ready To Play?

Now it’s your turn to try it out. Pick a number, start the game, and see how the pattern unfolds. Try and keep up, and figure out whether it's a Fizz, Buzz, or a FizzBuzz before we show it to you.
